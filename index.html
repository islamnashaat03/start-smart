Frequently Asked Questions

How long do I have access to the course materials?
Basic Plan members receive lifetime access to all course materials and 30 days of community access. Premium Plan members get 6 months of community access, and Executive Plan members receive lifetime access to both course materials and the community forum.
How is this program different from other business courses?
Most programs focus on just one area—either business fundamentals, marketing, sales, OR personal branding. Our academy integrates all four essential skill areas into one comprehensive curriculum, teaching you how these domains work together to create business success. This integrated approach provides much better results than learning each skill in isolation.
Do I need prior business experience to benefit from this program?
No prior business experience is required. The program is designed to benefit both beginners and experienced professionals. We start with fundamentals and progress to more advanced concepts, allowing everyone to learn at their own pace and apply knowledge to their unique situations.
What is the time commitment required?
The core curriculum takes approximately 40 hours to complete. Most students finish the program within 8-10 weeks by dedicating 4-5 hours per week. However, you can move at your own pace—some complete it faster, while others take more time to implement concepts thoroughly before moving on.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
Yes, we offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you complete the first two modules and don't find value in the program, we'll provide a full refund. We're confident in the quality of our curriculum and the results our students achieve.
Do you offer corporate training packages?
Yes, we have custom corporate training packages available for teams of 5 or more. These include tailored content specific to your industry, team assessments, and group coaching sessions. Please contact our team directly at corporate@businessskills.com for details and pricing.
